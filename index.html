The Community

Not a club.
A movement.

Runivo started with three people and a WhatsApp group. No funding, no fleet sponsors, no paid influencers. Just a shared belief that running is better when you're not alone.

We run early mornings, late evenings, and on days when it doesn't make sense to run. We've had first-timers complete their first 5K and veterans clock sub-4 marathons. The pace doesn't matter. Showing up does.

In two years, we've grown to over 1,000 runners across Bengaluru — entirely through word of mouth. Every member brought someone else. That's the only growth strategy we've ever had.
